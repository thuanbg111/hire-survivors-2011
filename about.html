AboutHire Survivors

Hire Hollywood Survivors is a U.S. 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN 991362011) formed to bring practical support to people in transition. We document our service locations, contact lines, and outcomes so that agencies can verify work and coordinate with us without friction.
